What We Offer!

Community

We welcome everyone, and we mean everyone. You don't have to a be a musician, you might just want to hang out at the bar or meet new people and we welcome that wholeheartely. You will meet some amazing people here at String Up and we can't wait to see you about!

Experience

We offer a large amount of experience and knowledge here at String Up. No questions is a silly question. Everyone has different levels of knowledge and we take great pride in educating you!

Events

We love our live music, duh, who doesn't. But we also love getting some amazing artists come into the shop and explain how they make the music they do. We have hosted some of the best artists in the world thanks to our partners, which you can read about more below. I

Services

We offer a lot of services at String Up. We will buy and sell instruments, we will fix and service your instruments. We also offer venue hire and corperate events hire too.

About

We are an independent venue started up in 2018. We are passionate about music and wanted to create a space for asipiring and experienced musicians to share and create. As well as gigs we help musicians however we can, whether it be setting up their instruments or answering any queries they may have.

Partners

Though we are independent our partners help make a lot of the workshops and gigs possible. We want to thank Fender, Gibson and Ernie Ball for all the support. To find out more about our partners click on their names!

Socials

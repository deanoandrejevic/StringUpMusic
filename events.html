Events and Workshops

An Auidence with Synyster Gates

30th July 2021

This is huge! We have the lead guitarist of Avenged Sevenfold coming in to our shop to talk about the bands music and his musical experiences. He will also be playing some of his favourite riffs and solos for all of you. If that wasn't good enough there will also be a Q&A session. So if you have any burning questions you want to ask then you'll get that chance.

You Me At Six

3rd August 2021

You Me At Six have just released their 7th studio album, "SUCKAPUNCH", and they will be spending an evening with us to celebrate such an event. This intimate gig is a real treat for all You Me At Six fans out there.

Big Ol' Jumble Sale

8th August 2021

Join us for our bi-monthly jumble sale, We will be selling everything from old records, music memoribilia, instruments and we will also be running a raffle for a special prize which will be announced sooner to the time.

Fender Workshop

14th August 2021

Our partners at Fender are coming down to hold a special workshop with you lot! They will be showing the latest models of Stratocasters and Telecasters. There will be hands on time with the guitars and there will also be the oppotunity to buy the new models at a discount price just for attending the event.

Spiritbox

18th August 2021

We are so excited to welcome Spiritbox! They are still hard at work writing their new album but we managed to snatch them away for a few short days to play here at String Up. We have also convinced them to play a couple of new songs for the VERY FIRST TIME! They will also be playing bangers like Holy Roller and Blessed Be. You don't want to miss this!

Gerry Cinnamon

25th August 2021

After riding high on the success of Erratic Cinematic, Gerry is about to start writing his new album, but before he does, he wanted to come in and play one last gig for you! So if you want to hear songs like Belter and Sometimes then make sure you snag tickets for this. Oh and as always there will be a short Q&A after the gig!

About

We are an independent venue started up in 2018. We are passionate about music and wanted to create a space for asipiring and experienced musicians to share and create. As well as gigs we help musicians however we can, whether it be setting up their instruments or answering any queries they may have.

Partners

Though we are independent our partners help make a lot of the workshops and gigs possible. We want to thank Fender, Gibson and Ernie Ball for all the support. To find out more about our partners click on their names!

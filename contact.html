Job Opportunities

We're hiring! Want to join the team? We have multiple positions available. Check them out them below! If any of the positions are of interest fill out the contact form below with the relavent information!

Sound Technican

As a sound technican you will be responsible for all the upkeep of the sound equipment we have on site. You will also help musicians with any quiries they may have in the shop and during any gigs. Some of the musicians may be inexperienced in setting up sound equipment when they come to play at String Up and you be the one they turn too and in helping them they may learn a thing or two also. If you think this role sounds good for you then apply below

Bar Staff

Being a part of the bar staff means you will part of an experienced and personable team. Your responsibility as a part of the bar staff will include serving drinks, keeping record of stock, and keeping the area around the bar tidy. As part of your job you will also be asked occassionaly to look after some of the musicians that come into play at String Up. If you think this role sounds good for you then apply below

Events Co-ordinator

Being the events co-ordinator is a huge role. You will be in charge of organizing workshops and gigs at String Up. You will be working closely with our partners too as they help find a lot of the talent that come to play here. If you think this role sounds good for you then apply below

Guitar repair Technican

You will be working in the workshop. This is a job that can be very rewarding. You will be helping customers diagnose and possibly fix their guitars. This can be anything from just simply changing strings to fixing guitar necks. You may also be asked to attend gigs to help the more inexperienced musicians set up their guitars for the gig. If you think this role sounds good for you then apply below

About

We are an independent venue started up in 2018. We are passionate about music and wanted to create a space for asipiring and experienced musicians to share and create. As well as gigs we help musicians however we can, whether it be setting up their instruments or answering any queries they may have.

Partners

Though we are independent our partners help make a lot of the workshops and gigs possible. We want to thank Fender, Gibson and Ernie Ball for all the support. To find out more about our partners click on their names!
